  • most affordable shortlisted areas with an outstanding primary school
  • cottages priced LISA-eligible within a 25-min commute
  • what would a +£500/mo savings boost do to my move date?
  • show me everything where the stretched payment stays under £1,900/mo

What this will do

  • Parse natural-language queries into structured filters over data/criteria.json, the areas index, and listings.
  • Return ranked results with the same fit-dot vocabulary used everywhere else in the app.
  • Cite the data it used — area ids, finance lines, criteria fields — so you can trust the answer.
  • Falls back gracefully when a query doesn’t resolve to anything.
