What this will do

  • Pull open property listings (sale + rentals) for every area on your shortlist.
  • Rank each listing with the same fit dot used on the dashboard and Areas page.
  • Surface chain-free status, price-paid history, council tax band, EPC, and LISA eligibility inline.
  • Saved listings persist locally; price drops or new matches trigger an in-app badge.
  • Filters mirror your criteria so the moment a fitting listing appears, it’s here.
