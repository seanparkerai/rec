Outreach

Pre-filled email drafts that turn shortlisted listings into viewings, brokers into appointments, and follow-ups into one click. Templates and example bodies below.

Drafts · pre-filled from your profile and the listing

Three templates

Viewing request

Subject: Viewing — <listing address>, this weekend

Polite opener, your two preferred slots, broker status, chain-free flag.

Mortgage broker intro

Subject: First-time buyer enquiry — <offer target>

Your finances summary, target price, deposit, moving window. Pulls from finances.

Post-viewing follow-up

Subject: Following up on <address>

Quick thanks, two follow-up questions, offer interest signal.

What this will do

  • mailto: drafts pre-filled from data/profile.json and the chosen listing or area.
  • Falls back to a one-tap copy if mailto: isn’t available.
  • Tracks status per outreach (drafted · sent · replied) via storage.js.
  • Pulls listing context from the Listings page once that ships.
