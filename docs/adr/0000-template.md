# NNNN. Title (imperative, one line)

Date: YYYY-MM-DD

## Status

Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by NNNN | Declined

## State / rail

The file(s), test(s), or contract the decision governs — usually one of the §4 rails.

## Context

What forces are at play — the problem, the constraints, the rail being changed and why the
current design is no longer the best one. Two or three paragraphs at most.

## Decision

What we decided, stated in full sentences in the active voice ("We will …"). Name the files,
tests, and ceremonies the decision touches.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, what must now be maintained, and what would trigger
revisiting this decision.
